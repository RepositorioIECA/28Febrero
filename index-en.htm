The Activity Indicators in the Services Sector is to obtain information on the evolution of market services in Andalusia (Trade, Tourism, Transport, Information Technology and Companies Services) by tracking three variables, turnover, employment and VAB.

  • Organization: Institute of Statistics and Cartography of Andalusia.
  • Scope: Andalusia.
